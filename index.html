FAQ

Questions Answered

What broker should I use?+

Any MT5 broker with Raw/Pro spread accounts. Exness, IC Markets, Pepperstone all work well. Avoid Standard accounts — spread eats profits. Max spread: 20 points on forex pairs.

What account size do I need?+

Minimum $500, recommended $1,000–$2,000. Backtest run on $2,000. Running all 5 pairs: set RiskPercent to 0.3% per pair (max 1.5% total exposure).

Does it work on FTMO and FundedNext?+

Yes — TITAN Pro is built for prop firm challenges. 4% daily DD hard stop (below FTMO's 5% rule). News blackout handles FOMC/NFP automatically. Buyer guide includes exact input settings per firm.

Do I need to watch it 24/7?+

No. Runs fully automated on a VPS. It monitors markets 24/5, manages all open trades, applies trailing stops, and closes everything Friday before market close.

Is this a martingale or grid system?+

No. Fixed lot sizing only. One position maximum per pair at any time. No doubling on losers. Hard daily DD cap and overall DD cap built in as code-level hard stops.

What VPS do I need?+

Any Windows VPS with MT5 installed. Hostinger VPS (~$8/mo), Contabo (~$5/mo), or ForexVPS.net work well. You host the VPS — TITAN runs inside your MT5 terminal.

Who built this EA?+

TITAN was built by Philina Kendricks (20+ years, forex & gold) and Philip John (30+ years, forex & commodities). Two private traders with 50+ combined years of live market experience. See the Founders section above.

Which currency pairs does TITAN trade?+

TITAN trades exactly 5 pairs on the H1 timeframe: USDCHF, GBPUSD, AUDUSD, AUDCHF, and CADCHF. These were selected after testing 22 pairs — only pairs with verified profit factors above 1.44 across a 17-month window made the cut. EURUSD and JPY pairs are permanently excluded.

What is the difference between TITAN and TITAN Pro?+

TITAN AutoTrader ($250) is optimised for live retail accounts — standard risk management, all 5 pairs. TITAN AutoTrader Pro ($399) is the prop firm edition: it adds a 4% hard daily drawdown stop, news blackout on FOMC/NFP events, and a Friday cut-off close. It is specifically engineered to pass FTMO and FundedNext Phase 1 and Phase 2 challenges. The Suite ($549) includes both versions plus Inner Circle access.

How was the backtest conducted?+

All results use MT5 Strategy Tester with 1-minute OHLC data and 100% modelling quality. Starting balance: $2,000. Period: 17 months covering bull, bear, and sideways regimes. No look-ahead bias — each trade entry, management, and exit is evaluated tick-by-tick. Spread is factored in on every trade.

Does TITAN trade during news events?+

TITAN Pro includes an automatic news blackout that prevents new entries 30 minutes before and after high-impact events (FOMC, NFP, CPI). All open trades are managed normally during news — only new entries are blocked. The standard TITAN version does not include this filter, making it suited for brokers who allow news trading.

Can I run TITAN on multiple accounts?+

Each purchase licenses TITAN for use on one live account at a time. If you want to run it on a second account (e.g., a funded prop account alongside your personal account), you would need an additional license. Contact us via the support channel after purchase to discuss multi-account options.

What happens if my VPS disconnects?+

Any open trades placed by TITAN remain on your broker's server — they do not close when MT5 disconnects. However, TITAN will not be able to manage trailing stops or apply the daily DD cap until MT5 reconnects. We recommend a reliable VPS with 99.9%+ uptime and setting a broker-side stop loss on all open trades as a safety net.

Can I adjust the risk settings?+

Yes. RiskPercent is the primary input — it determines position size as a percentage of account balance per trade. The recommended starting setting is 1% per pair. Running all 5 pairs simultaneously, we suggest 0.3% per pair (1.5% total exposure). You can also adjust ATR multipliers for stop loss and trailing stop if you want tighter or wider exits.

Do I get free updates?+

Yes — all updates are free, forever. Markets evolve and so does TITAN. When we release an improved version, all existing buyers receive the updated .mq5 file by email automatically. No upgrade fees. No expiry. No re-purchase required. This includes both performance improvements and broker compatibility fixes.
