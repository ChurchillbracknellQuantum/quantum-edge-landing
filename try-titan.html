Don't have MetaTrader 5 installed yet?

MT5 (MetaTrader 5) is the free trading platform that runs the TITAN EA. You need it installed before you can do anything below. It is completely free, takes 2 minutes to install, and you do not need to deposit any money.

⬇  Download MetaTrader 5 — Free Windows only · Official MetaQuotes download · No deposit required

Already have MT5? Skip this and go straight to Step 1 below.

1

Copy the EA into MT5

In MT5 go to File → Open Data Folder → MQL5 → Experts. Copy TITAN_DEMO.ex5 into that folder. Restart MT5 so it appears in the Expert Advisors list.

2

Allow one quick setting

Go to Tools → Options → Expert Advisors tab. Tick "Allow WebRequests for listed URL" and add https://getquantumedgeai.com. Click OK. This lets the EA verify its free trial — done once, saves permanently.

3

Open Strategy Tester

Press Ctrl + R in MT5. The Strategy Tester panel opens at the bottom of your screen. This is where the backtest runs.

4

Set these exact parameters

Expert: QuantumForex_TITAN_DEMO
Symbol: CADCHF
Timeframe: H1
Model: 1 Minute OHLC
From: 2025.01.01
To: 2026.05.31
Deposit: 2000 USD

5

Check Inputs tab

Click the Inputs tab. Confirm RiskPercent = 1.0. Leave all other values at default — do not change anything else.

6

Start — then read the Report

Click Start. Finishes in under a minute. When the progress bar completes, click the Report tab to see Win Rate, Profit Factor, Drawdown, and every trade.
